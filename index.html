GENERAL
The Summit Awards recognize outstanding performance across the Kauffman FastTrac affiliate network. Each award highlights a different dimension of excellence, from program volume and long-term commitment to ecosystem building. Awards are presented at the annual Summit in Kansas City, MO on July 28-30, 2026.
All awards are open to active FastTrac affiliates, with one exception: the Rising Star Affiliate Award is limited to affiliates who have been active for three years or fewer. The Outstanding Facilitator Award recognizes an individual facilitator rather than an organization.
For the data-driven awards (High Impact, Sustained Excellence, Rising Star, and Ecosystem Builder), no application is required. Winners are determined using cohort delivery and activity data pulled directly from our LMS and CRM. For the Outstanding Facilitator Award, a nomination must be submitted by a Program Director, Course Manager, or Fellow Facilitator.
An affiliate can be considered for multiple awards. Each award measures something different, so it is possible for the same organization to qualify for more than one recognition in the same year.
THE AWARDS
This award recognizes the affiliate in each region that delivered the highest number of FastTrac cohorts in the past 12 months. It is about program delivery at scale. Winners are selected separately for the East Region and West Region. If there is a tie, the tiebreaker is total cohorts delivered over the affiliate's lifetime.
This award honors long-term commitment combined with consistent delivery. It is calculated using a composite score: Years Active multiplied by Total Cohorts Delivered. It rewards affiliates that have made FastTrac a sustained, reliable presence in their community, not a one-time experiment. Winners are selected separately for the East Region and West Region.
This award recognizes newer affiliates, those active for three years or fewer, who are already making a meaningful impact. Rather than measuring total volume, it measures pace: cohorts delivered divided by years active. A newer affiliate that hits the ground running has a real shot at this one. Winners are selected separately for the East Region and West Region.
This award is presented to every FastTrac affiliate that also operates an active 1 Million Cups community. It is not a competition with a single winner — every qualifying affiliate is recognized for their commitment to building a full entrepreneurship ecosystem. If your organization runs both FastTrac and a 1MC community, you will receive this award.
This individual-level award recognizes FastTrac facilitators who create an engaging, memorable cohort experience, bring additional resources and connections into every session, and go above and beyond to connect entrepreneurs to each other and the broader ecosystem. Two awards are given per region. Nominees must be submitted by a Program Director, Course Manager, or Fellow Facilitator.
